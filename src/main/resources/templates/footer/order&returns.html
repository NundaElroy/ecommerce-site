Orders & Returns

Order Processing:

All orders placed with Electro Uganda are processed within 24 hours and shipped within 2-5 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been dispatched.

Returns & Refunds:

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you need to return an item, please ensure:

  • The item is in its original packaging.
  • A return request is made within 7 days of delivery.
  • The product is unused and in resalable condition.

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after the returned item is inspected.

For returns, email us at support@electro.co.ug or call +256 700 123456.
